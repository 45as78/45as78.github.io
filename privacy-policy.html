Tunely Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Tunely ("we," "our," or "the app") is a guitar tuner and music practice tool for Android. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data Tunely accesses, what we collect, and how it is used.

Microphone Access

Tunely requires microphone access to detect the pitch of your instrument in real time. Audio is processed entirely on your device. We do not record, store, transmit, or share any audio data. No audio ever leaves your phone.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Tunely does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email, and does not track your location. We do not have servers that receive data from the app.

Advertising

Tunely displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads, including device identifiers, IP address, and general usage data. This data collection is governed by Google's privacy policy.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use their services at Google's Partner Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

In-App Purchases

Tunely may offer premium features through in-app purchases processed by Google Play. Payment information is handled entirely by Google Play and is not accessible to us. We do not collect, store, or process any payment or billing information.

Data Shared with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data. The only third-party service integrated into Tunely is Google AdMob for advertising, which may collect device-level data as described above.

Children's Privacy

Tunely is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app's ad implementation follows Google's policies for general audience apps.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no user data at risk. All audio processing happens locally on your device and is discarded immediately after pitch detection.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
